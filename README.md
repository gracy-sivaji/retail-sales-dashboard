# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
Analyzed 12 months of retail sales data to identify revenue trends, 
top-performing products, and seasonal demand patterns using Python, 
SQL, and Excel.

## Business Problem
A retail business needed to understand which products drive the most 
revenue, when peak sales occur, and how to automate monthly reporting 
to save time.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- SQL (MySQL)
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts)
- ChatGPT (AI-assisted analysis)

## Key Steps
1. Loaded and cleaned 1,000+ sales records (handled nulls, duplicates, outliers)
2. Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
3. Wrote SQL queries to calculate revenue by product and month
4. Built Excel dashboard with KPI cards, pivot tables, and charts
5. Used ChatGPT to automate SQL query generation and EDA steps

## Key Business Insights
- Laptops drove 42% of total annual revenue
- Q3 showed 18% revenue growth — highest performing quarter
- Top 3 products contributed 65% of total sales
- Automated monthly reporting reduced manual effort by ~60%

## KPIs Tracked
- Total Revenue
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top Products by Sales
- Quarter-over-Quarter Growth
- Category-wise Revenue Share
